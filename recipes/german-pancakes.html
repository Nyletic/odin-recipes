German pancakes

Description

A German pancake (a.k.a. a Dutch baby) is similar to a large Yorkshire pudding. Unlike other types of pancakes, German pancakes are baked in the oven instead of fried on the stove. They also donâ€™t contain leaving ingredients, such as baking powder or baking soda.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Melt butter in a medium baking dish.
  3. Mix flour, milk, eggs, and salt in a medium bowl.
  4. Pour the mixture into the prepared baking dish.
  5. Bake on center rack in the preheated oven for 30 to 40 minutes, until golden brown.
